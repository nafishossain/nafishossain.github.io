Who We Are

Shrimpex Agencies Limited is a registered company specializing in the seafood buying agency business, with extensive experience and a strong reputation in this field.

As a buying agent, the company performs the following tasks on behalf of its principals:

  • Selects reputed and reliable packers
  • Conducts regular pre-shipment inspections through qualified Q.C. inspectors
  • Performs surprise supervision of the day-to-day production processes at packers where buying contracts are established
  • Ensures timely shipments
  • Provides market information related to Bangladesh to its principals

In short, we are always ready to ease your concerns and act as your trusted eyes and ears in the country of origin.

Currently, we are seeking shrimp importers interested in importing frozen shrimp and seafood from Bangladesh. We work exclusively with HACCP-compliant packers in Bangladesh to ensure the highest quality standards.
